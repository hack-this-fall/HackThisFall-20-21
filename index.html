WHAT IS HACK THIS FALL?

Read Out Loud.


Hack This Fall is a 24 Hours Virtual Hackathon in which we aim to promote Hackers and help them nurture their new ideas and prototypes in various fields of tech. This can help us to solve the shared problems of our society and bring a change in a revolutionary way.
Here, we will talk about various technologies, create awareness, encourage others, Hack to contribute and many more exciting events & sessions.
We have various tracks under which you can choose your hack. Check it here

What is a Hackathon?

Hackathons are an incredible way to bring your community together to learn new skills, build amazing projects, and share ideas. A hackathon is best described as an “invention marathon”. Anyone who has an interest in technology attends a hackathon to learn, build & share their creations over the course of a weekend in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. One don’t have to be a programmer and one certainly don’t have to be majoring in Computer Science!

    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    Hack This Fall is a 24 Hours Virtual Hackathon in which we aim to promote Hackers and help them nurture their new ideas and prototypes in various fields of tech. This can help us to solve the shared problems of our society and bring a change in a revolutionary way.
    Hack This Fall is a virtual 24-hours hackathon on 31st October-1st November, 2020.
    All students including undergraduate, graduate and high school students with any background are welcome!
    No experience is necessary. We will have plenty of mentors and resources available, along with several workshops targeted for beginners. Come learn and experience your first hackathon at Hack This Fall!
    The developer/developers of the web/mobile application will have all rights and own the IP of the project. However, all code needs to be in public domain (open source) so that it can be evaluated by the judges.
    Hack This Fall is 100% free. You don’t have to spend anything!
    Of course you can! We encourage people to work in teams of up to 4 people. You may opt-in to team formation through our Discord Server which will match you with an ideal team. You can work alone as well.
    We apologize, but all hackathon's projects should be developed during the event from scratch. We want honest conditions for all hackers. That's why all code should be done during the hackathon.
    We want to ensure a positive experience for all participants. We will be following MLH's Code of Conduct, we encourage you to read it here.
    You can always contact us at contact@hackthisfall.tech and we would be more than happy to help you.
